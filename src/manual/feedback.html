Feedback and Troubleshooting

If things do not work, especially simple things like bibant -version, then something is wrong with your configuration. Before filing bug reports or emailing us, please do:

  1. Check your environment variables. Is JAVA_HOME correct and pointing to a valid JRE / JDK (>= 1.5)? If it has quotes or trailing slashes, remove them.
  2. Is your PATH correct? Is BibAnt on it? What about JRE/bin or JDK/bin? If you've installed BibAnt for the first time with the option checked to add BibAnt to the PATH (this is the default), have you restarted your computer? This is sometimes required to get the new PATH definition taken in account.
  3. If a task doesn't do what you expect, run bibant -verbose or bibant -debug to see what is happening.

If you can't fix your problem, send us an email.

If you think you've found a bug and are a registered customer, you can report it to our Issue Tracking System.

If you're struggling to develop a BibAnt script to support your documentation process, do email us, we're here to help!
